How to use

More Information

Our AFL platform is designed for simplicity and ease of use.

First choose your settings. Fast calculation will mean the fuzzer will make more mistakes on average, but will run significantly quicker.


The selected tag will change how the results are presented on the live metrics page, but will not affact the fuzzing performance.

And start Fuzzing

You can monitor your progress from the Live Metrics page.

Each session will run to completion, exiting if it fails to find more results

Previous jobs can be found on Job History page.
